# Water Tank Card

A Lovelace card that shows the water level of a tank as a styled vessel with an animated wavy surface, a side scale, litres readout, status pill, optional temperature chip, and an optional pump control with flow animation and a fault warning.

Built for the Rototec NPI4000 tank (Ø171 · H215 · 4050 L) but works with any tank — set your own capacity, colours and thresholds. Configurable entirely from the UI editor or YAML.
